# *Online Safety Tips💻*

*1.* *Privacy First:* Set all social media profiles to 'Private' and only accept requests from people you know personally. College students who post frequently on Instagram must necessarily set their profiles on private as they might have the risk of being tracked by some stranger and then get bullied by cyberbullies.  

*2.   Think Before You Click:* Avoid clicking on suspicious links in emails or messages, even if they appear to be from friends. Always if possible ask them personally either by physically or on a call if they have sent a link or not.  

*3.*   *Screen Before Sharing:* Never share your OTP, password, or sensitive personal documents on public or semi-public forums.  

*4.   Digital Footprint:* Be mindful that anything you post can be screenshotted and shared; maintain a professional digital image. A single mistake noted by a cyber criminal can be harmful for your online presence and these days even can affect your career stats.  

*5.   UPI Safety :* Never enter your UPI PIN to receive money, a PIN is only required for sending or authorizing a payment.  

*6.   UPI Safety :* Always verify the merchant's name on the payment screen before hitting 'Pay' to avoid QR code tampering scams.  

*7.   Evidence Collection:* If harassed, do not delete the messages, take screenshots as evidence for reporting. These evidences can be used by the police departments to track the digital footprints and then trace the bully and punish him accordingly.   

*8.   Secure Your Accounts:* Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) on all academic and personal accounts. This sends a notification on your phone or known devices whether to approve a device to login and access your account or not. A single no can cancel the hacker to access your account and steal your information  

*9.* In case you become avictim, dont get scared and instantly report to the *National Cyber Crime Portal  (cybercrime.gov.in)*  and if immediate action needs to be taken then call the *Helpline number 1930*  for immediate action that can be taken against the cyber bully
