# DO'S ✅
* Audit Your Digital Footprint: Regularly check your privacy settings and remove any unprofessional content from your past.
* Verify Before You Share: Fact-check news or academic rumors before reposting to prevent the spread of misinformation. Do not make any false news viral and spread rumours.
* Network Professionally: Use platforms like LinkedIn to follow industry leaders and VIT Bhopal alumni in the CSE field.
* Support Your Peers: Use your platform to highlight team projects, hackathon wins, and college club achievements. Obtain the certifications and upload them in LinkedIn posts for a better career portfolio.
* Think Before You Post: Ask yourself,  "Will I  be comfortable if a future recruiter or professor saw this post?" before posting anything on your career portfolio platforms like LinkedIn, GitHub, etc.

# DONT'S❌
* Don't Over-Share Personal Info: Never post your registration number, OTPs, home address, or private phone numbers.
* Don't Engage with Trolls: Avoid heated public arguments or "cyber-wars" that could stay on your digital record as they could ruin your career if some serious arguments are happening and if your history is evaluated. 
* Don't Plagiarize Content: Always give credit when sharing someone else's code, design, or research. 
* Don't Post During Lectures: Avoid posting "in-the-moment" content that shows you are distracted during academic hours.
* Don't Use Unprofessional Handles: Avoid using "cool" or "edgy" usernames for accounts linked to your professional portfolio.
